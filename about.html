About Samantha

Samantha is an event coordinator for the Disney Event Group who has a passion for curating magical experiences and making dreams come true. With strong attention to detail and a creative perspective, she strives to make every event a long-lasting memory with extraordinary moments. Beyond her professional life, she brings her strong personal drive and passion to her daily life. She is committed to volunteer work and sustainability, trying to improve her community and the world around her. She also enjoys getting lost in the pages of books, expanding her creativity with crochet and various crafts, and immersing herself in cozy video games.

Stories and creativity play vital roles in all aspects of her life. The ability to experience a story, whether it's from the pages of a book or from the captivating virtual worlds in video games, inspires creativity throughout her life. Being engrossed in realms created by these stories' fuels her ability to brainstorm the magic that she brings to her events. This creative energy also flows into her enjoyment of creating things through crafts, especially through crochet. The combination of having a creative outlet and interacting with stories provides a great escape from the event planning world when a break is needed as well.

This blend of stories, creativity, and passion allow her to bring a unique perspective to her career as an event coordinator. Her dedication to creating that unforgettable memory that brings a moment of magic into reality truly makes her events an enchanting experience.
